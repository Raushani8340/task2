My City

Patna, the capital of Bihar, is a city rich in history and culture. Situated on the banks of the Ganga River, it has been a significant center of learning and politics for centuries. From its ancient roots as Pataliputra, the Mauryan capital, to its modern-day developments, Patna blends tradition with progress.

The city boasts historical landmarks like the Bihar Museum, Agamkuan, and Takht Sri Patna Sahib, reflecting its diverse heritage. Patna is also known for its vibrant festivals, especially Chhath Puja, and its unique cuisine like Litti Chhokha.

As a hub of education and governance, Patna plays a crucial role in Bihar's growth. Its blend of ancient history and modern aspirations makes it a fascinating city to explore. Patna stands as a testament to Bihar's rich past and its promising future.
